Aitys with Artificial Intelligence

A new form of folk art where Kazakh tradition meets modern innovation.

What is Aitys?

  • Traditional Kazakh oral folk art
  • Competition between two akyns improvising verses
  • Main themes: love, nature, society, humor

Aitys & Technologies

Artificial intelligence brings new dimensions to the ancient art. AI can compose rhymes, improvise, and even interact with a live akyn in real-time, keeping Aitys alive in the digital age.

How AI Works

  1. Trained on large poetry and text databases.
  2. Generates rhymes and thematic lines.
  3. Reacts to live akyns in real-time competitions.

Advantages

  • New creative possibilities.
  • Helps young akyns learn faster.
  • Fun for viewers and schools.
  • Preserves Kazakh traditions digitally.

Examples

  • AI poem: “Nature and the City.”
  • Virtual akyn vs live akyn battle.
  • Interactive online Aitys performances.

Conclusion

Aitys with AI is where tradition and technology merge. It’s engaging, educational, and inspiring — the future of Kazakh folk art in a digital form.
